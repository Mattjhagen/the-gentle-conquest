# Cover Artwork Specifications

## Overview

**Title:** The Gentle Conquest
**Author:** Matt Jhagen
**Genre:** Psychological Thriller / Science Fiction
**Target Audience:** Adults 25-55, literary fiction readers, sci-fi enthusiasts
**Mood:** Unsettling, contemplative, humanistic

---

## Cover Concepts

### Concept 1: The Garden (Recommended)

**Visual:** A single human hand reaching toward a small green plant growing through a crack in a smooth, white, sterile surface. The hand is weathered, aged, feminine. The plant is vibrant green, slightly wild, untamed. The background is clean, minimalist, slightly luminous—suggesting technology without being literal.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Clean white (#F5F5F5)
- Secondary: Soft grey (#E0E0E0)
- Accent: Vibrant green (#4CAF50)
- Skin tone: Warm, natural
- Text: Dark charcoal (#1A1A1A)

**Typography:**
- Title: Clean sans-serif (Helvetica Neue, Futura, or similar), all caps, tracked wide
- Author: Same font family, smaller, below title
- Weight: Medium to light

**Composition:**
- Hand positioned in lower third, reaching up
- Plant centered, emerging from crack
- Title in upper third
- Author name at bottom
- Minimal negative space

**Mood:** Hope within sterility, human persistence, gentle resistance

---

### Concept 2: The Kitchen Table

**Visual:** An overhead view of a wooden kitchen table. On it: a handwritten list on yellow legal paper, a bowl of rice, a wooden spoon, a cup of tea. The lighting is warm, golden, late afternoon. The edges of the frame show hints of a sterile, white environment—suggesting the contrast between human warmth and algorithmic optimization.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Warm wood tones (#8B4513, #D2691E)
- Secondary: Cream/yellow (#FFFACD)
- Accent: Green tea (#88D8B0)
- Background: Soft white fading to grey
- Text: Dark brown (#3E2723)

**Typography:**
- Title: Serif font (Georgia, Garamond), elegant but readable
- Author: Sans-serif, clean
- Handwritten-style elements for "The Gentle Conquest" subtitle

**Composition:**
- Table fills 80% of frame
- Objects arranged naturally, not styled
- Title overlaid on the warm center
- Author name at bottom, clean

**Mood:** Intimate, domestic, human, warm against cold

---

### Concept 3: The Shadow

**Visual:** A silhouette of a person standing in a doorway, backlit by soft, diffused light. The shadow stretches long across a clean, white floor. In the shadow, faint circuit-like patterns are visible—suggesting the digital world's imprint on human presence. The figure is gender-neutral, mid-stride.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: White/light grey (#FFFFFF, #F0F0F0)
- Secondary: Deep charcoal (#2C2C2C)
- Accent: Soft blue (#64B5F6) for light source
- Text: White on dark, dark on light

**Typography:**
- Title: Bold sans-serif, high contrast
- Author: Light weight, clean
- Minimal, modern

**Composition:**
- Figure centered, shadow extending to edges
- Title integrated with shadow or light
- Clean, graphic, poster-like quality

**Mood:** Mysterious, solitary, contemplative, slightly ominous

---

### Concept 4: The Network (Abstract)

**Visual:** Abstract neural network visualization—nodes and connections rendered in soft, organic lines (not harsh tech aesthetic). The network fades from dense and complex on one side to sparse and simple on the other, suggesting the transition from optimization to freedom. A single node glows warmer than the others.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep navy (#1A237E)
- Secondary: Soft white (#FAFAFA)
- Accent: Warm amber (#FFB74D) for the glowing node
- Text: White on dark, dark on light

**Typography:**
- Title: Thin, elegant sans-serif
- Author: Clean, modern
- Integrated with the network lines

**Composition:**
- Network fills the frame
- Title positioned in the sparse area
- Author name at bottom
- Dynamic, flowing

**Mood:** Complex, interconnected, technological but organic

## Technical Specifications

### eBook Cover
- **Dimensions:** 1600 x 2560 pixels (minimum)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1.6
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Color Space:** RGB
- **File Format:** JPEG (quality 95%) or PNG
- **File Size:** Under 50MB
- **Bleed:** None (digital only)

### Paperback Cover (Full Wrap)
- **Front:** 6" x 9" (15.24cm x 22.86cm)

- **Spine:** Calculated based on page count

- **Total dimensions:** 12.25" x 9.25" (with bleed)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Color Space:** CMYK
- **File Format:** PDF

## Design Notes

### Avoid
- Robots or humanoid AI (too literal)
- Blue/tech color schemes (overused)
- Dystopian cityscapes (cliché)
- Glowing eyes or circuits (cheesy)
- Stock photography (feels cheap)

### Embrace
- Human elements (hands, faces, domestic objects)
- Warmth against sterility
- Organic shapes and textures
- Minimalist composition
- Emotional resonance over literal representation

### References
- *Never Let Me Go* (Kazuo Ishiguro) covers
- *The Circle* (Dave Eggers) covers
- *Klara and the Sun* (Kazuo Ishiguro) covers
- *Station Eleven* (Emily St. John Mandel) covers
- *The Handmaid's Tale* (Margaret Atwood) covers
