# Categories

## Amazon Categories (3 max)

### Primary Category
- **Kindle Store > Kindle eBooks > Science Fiction & Fantasy > Science Fiction > Time Travel**
  - *Note: Time Travel is a proxy for "near-future" or "speculative" fiction, which Amazon doesn't have as a direct category. Alternative: Science Fiction > Dystopian.*

### Secondary Category
- **Kindle Store > Kindle eBooks > Literature & Fiction > Genre Fiction > Psychological**
  - *Captures the psychological thriller aspect of the novel.*

### Tertiary Category
- **Kindle Store > Kindle eBooks > Science Fiction & Fantasy > Science Fiction > Technological**
  - *Captures the AI/technology focus.*

## Notes

- Amazon allows 3 Browse Categories for eBooks
- Categories affect visibility in "Best Sellers" lists
- Choose categories where the book can realistically rank
- Avoid overly broad categories (e.g., "Science Fiction" alone)
- Consider niche categories for better visibility
