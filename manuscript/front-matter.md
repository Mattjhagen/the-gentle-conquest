## A Note on This Novel

*The Gentle Conquest* is a work of speculative fiction. While it draws on real trends in artificial intelligence, behavioral optimization, and neuroscience, all characters, organizations, and specific technologies are fictional. The novel is intended as a thought experiment, not a prediction.

Any resemblance to actual persons, living or dead, or actual events is purely coincidental.
